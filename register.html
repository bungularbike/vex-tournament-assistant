Welcome to VEX Tournament Assistant!

VEX Tournament Assistant is a scouting helper for VEX Robotics Competition (VRC) tournaments. It can let you take notes on teams at your events, predict the outcome of matches, and compile data across multiple competitions a team has attended.

VEX Tournament Assistant is developed by Team 936A from Hong Kong International School. We will never access your data (including your e-mail address and team notes) and/or transfer it to other parties.

VRC, VEX, and VEX Robotics are trademarks of Innovation First International, Inc. All data compiled by VEX Tournament Assistant is from the publicly accessible VexDB API.
